My dear ALLII,

First of all, a very happy birthday ALLII (potti)—my favorite person, the one who always makes my heart feel safe, my safe space, and my happy place. Apart from that being said, I want to say something now. You are not just a friend, but a partner in crime. You're my therapist, and sometimes I'm yours. Sometimes we annoy the hell out of each other, and sometimes you annoy the hell out of me—because you never miss a chance to annoy me, roast me, and make me feel like I want to slap you. But I can't, because who else will?

There are so many things between us—where we laughed, cried, shared jokes, completely stupid moments, and endless laughs. From sharing secrets to making unforgettable memories. Sure, we have our moments of disagreement, but what is our friendship if we don't have those sometimes? We always find our way back to each other and fix things. Mainly, however, the person I am today is because of you. You are there for me in my ups and downs, and you have never left or turned away. Whenever life feels a bit too loud or confusing, just talking to you makes it all drift away.

I am lucky to have found someone who feels like home, the warmest light on the darkest days. You are kind, lovely, and sometimes childish. This is who you are—never, ever lose that part. If you lose that childish part, then I would say you have lost yourself completely. That is what I like about you. Lately, I realized that spending time with you is the only thing I have not regretted—not even a thought of regret. I am happiest around you. You can annoy me, but I'd spend every split second near you and never get tired. Imagine how boring your life would be if I didn't have to annoy you!

Now, all of this aside, I want to say that our teenage years are done. We've graduated, and we are now in adulthood—a hard reality where we constantly chase our dreams to come true. Eventually, we face so many challenges, have our highs and lows. Many people will say so many things, and we have to face them because we are not just kids anymore, and we have responsibilities on our hands. We have to face the world.

So many people will say things to bring us down—those people may be friends, relatives, or family. We may feel like we have failed, and our hearts might feel heavy. But do not care about them, because you are chasing your dream to succeed. The path you have taken is not an easy one to achieve; it takes hard work, sleepless nights, and you will face many roadblocks. I trust you will break every roadblock and achieve your dream. When you achieve your dream, I will be there to celebrate your success. When you succeed, everyone will come to you—even the ones who said bad things will come like dogs, if I may say so.

Despite all that chaos, never lose yourself. Never lose your spark. Whether it's a good time or a bad time, I will always be there for you. That being said, there will also come a time when we may get a little busy. In those times we may talk less, or feel distant. We may or may not realize it, but in the end, it is important to not lose our bond just because life gets busy. Whenever needed, I will always be there.

Remember to always take a break sometimes and do not get overstressed or overwork yourself more than you can handle. Not just a break, but a small escape, maybe for a few days. Everything will happen; it just takes some time. The things that are supposed to happen will always find a way to happen. Sometimes they make sense, sometimes they don't. And sometimes they really, really hurt. But it's okay, because those things put us on a path to becoming who we are meant to be. And this is who we are meant to be. This is life. So, do not regret any moment of it—live to the fullest. Enjoy the day to the fullest, potti. Happy Birthday again ALLIII PILLIII.
